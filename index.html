Who We Are

Built For Champions

The SRC Sports Academy is a university-backed initiative founded to nurture athletic talent and promote a culture of discipline, teamwork, and excellence within Pentecost University and the wider community.

With professional coaching staff, modern facilities, and 10 sports disciplines, we provide every student and community member with a pathway to sport — regardless of experience level.
