Wrestling

One of the world's oldest combat sports, wrestling develops full-body strength, explosive power, and mental toughness. Our programme covers both Freestyle and Greco-Roman styles under certified national federation coaches.

Taekwondo

A Korean martial art famed for its emphasis on head-height kicks, spinning jump kicks, and fast kicking techniques. Taekwondo is an Olympic sport that builds flexibility, speed, discipline, and self-confidence.
