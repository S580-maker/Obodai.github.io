Overview

Who We Are

The SRC Sports Academy was established by the Student Representative Council of Pentecost University to create a structured, professional environment where students can pursue athletic excellence alongside their academic journey.

With backing from university funding bodies and regional sports organisations, we have grown from a small recreation programme into a fully-fledged sports institution offering 10 disciplines, certified coaching staff, and competitive pathways at regional and national levels.

Our doors are open not just to enrolled students, but to the surrounding community — because we believe that sport is a universal language that builds stronger, healthier, more connected societies.

Core Principles

Mission, Vision & Values

01

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality sports training and development opportunities to Pentecost University students and the broader community, fostering physical wellness, leadership, and competitive excellence.

02

Our Vision

To become the leading regional sports institution in Ghana, renowned for producing disciplined, skilled, and community-minded athletes who represent the values of Pentecost University on every stage.

🏆

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in coaching, competition, and personal development — settling for nothing less than our best.

🤝

Teamwork

Sport teaches us that individual success is built on collective effort. We train together, win together, and grow together.

🌍

Community

Our academy belongs to the community. We actively open our facilities and programmes to all who wish to participate.

🎯

Discipline

Athletic achievement demands focus, consistency, and dedication — values that extend beyond the field into every area of life.

♿

Inclusivity

Sport is for everyone. We welcome athletes of all abilities, backgrounds, and experience levels without discrimination.

🔬

Innovation

We embrace modern training methodologies, sports science, and technology to give our athletes every competitive advantage.

Support

Our Funding Sources

The academy is sustained through a combination of institutional support, sporting body partnerships, and community contributions.

🏛️

Pentecost University

Primary institutional funding covering facilities, staffing, and equipment.

🏅

Ghana Sports Authority

Government sports body providing grants for new discipline development.

🧘

National Sports Federations

Taekwondo, Wrestling & Volleyball federations providing coaching support.

🏘️

Community Partners

Local businesses and community organisations supporting events and sponsorships.

📝

SRC Student Levy

A small portion of student activity fees is allocated to the academy's operations.
